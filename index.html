We are a non-profit service group located in Hanoi, Vietnam, with an objective to empower the lives of entrepreneurial yet socioeconomically excluded women in rural provinces of Vietnam such as Hoa Binh through microloans, which provides them the financial capital to start, enhance, or sustain their businesses. Our inception can be dated back to 2013 in which we were able to initiate our services thanks to the UNIS Birthday Gift Fund's grant of $10,000. Since then, we have hosted pop-up shops, game nights, and other local events to finance our services and promote our cause at the UNIS community and Hanoi at large.
